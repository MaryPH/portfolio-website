Amazon Earn Coins feature


Description of image



Project description:

Amazon Coins is a digital currency that allows players to purchase apps, games and in-app items from the Amazon Appstore at a discounted price. Players can also earn free coins to use by purchasing other apps or in-app items. This project entailed creating an area in which customers could see all the options for earning free coins across apps, movies, music, books and more. This feature was design to be accessible on desktop, tablet and mobile devices.

My roles: UX Design, User research
