Amazon Gift Coins



Project description:

Amazon Coins is a digital currency that allows players to purchase apps, games and in-app items from the Amazon Appstore at a discounted price. This feature allowed for customers to purchase a bundle of coins and give them as a gift to someone else for use. This feature is accessible on desktop, tablet and mobile devices.
