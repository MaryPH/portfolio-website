About

Hello, my name is Mary Holmes.

I am a UX designer with over 20 years in the industry which include my previous roles as graphic designer and photographer. I’ve had the pleasure to work with many companies in different fields ranging from leading edge technology companies, a notable game company, home builders, and small design firms focused on small to medium size businesses. I’ve been fortunate and am energized to work with companies and clients that share my passion for creating engaging and delightful customer experiences that solve everyday challenges.

When I’m not at work, I’m either outdoors (camping, running, gardening, or hiking) or indoors in front of a screen (watching TV or surfing the internet). I am passionate about photography and usually have my iPhone ready to capture an image at a moments notice - much to the annoyance of folks around me.
